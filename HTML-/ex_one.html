1. <!DOCTYPE html>

You'll need this tag at the begining of every HTML document you create. It ensure that a
browser knows that it's reading HTML 5, the latest version.

Even though this isn't actually an HTML tag, it's still a good one to know.

2.<html>

This is another that a browser that it's reading HTML. The <html> tag goes
straight after the <DOCTYPE> tag, and you choose it with a <html> tag at the end of your
file Everthing else in your document goes between this tags.

3.<head>

The <head> tag the header section of file. The stuff that goes in here doesn't
appear in your website. Instead, it contains metadata for search engine and, into for
your browser.
